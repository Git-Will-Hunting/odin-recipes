Lasagna Recipe

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Spinach

Steps

  1. Boil lasagna noodles for 7 minutes and drain them
  2. Cook beef to 160°F
  3. Grease baking dish
  4. Mix ricotta cheese & spinach
  5. Add a thin layer of tomato sauce to the bottom of the dish
  6. Layer the ingredients in the following order:
    • Lasagna noodles
    • Tomato sauce
    • Beef
    • Cheese & spinach mixture
  7. top with Parmesan and Mozzarella cheese
  8. Cook covered with foil at 350°F for 30 minutes
  9. Cook uncovered for 10 minutes or until top browns
